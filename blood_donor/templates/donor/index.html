Why Donate Blood?

Make a Significant Impact

Blood cannot be manufactured, and the need for transfusions is constant. Your donation can help patients with accidents, surgeries, and medical conditions.

The Need is Universal

Blood transfusions are required by people of all ages and backgrounds. You never know when you or someone you love might need them.

Simple and Rewarding Process

Donating blood is a safe and relatively quick process. It's a small act of kindness that can make a world of difference.

Am I Eligible to Donate?

Most healthy individuals between the ages of 18-70 (or depending on local regulations) can donate blood. Here are some basic eligibility requirements:

  • Meet the weight and height requirements.
  • Be in good general health.
  • Have not donated blood recently (check eligibility period).
  • Not have certain medical conditions or medications.
See Full Eligibility Criteria

How to Donate Blood?

  1. Register as a donor using our quick and easy online form.
  2. Arrive at your appointment well-hydrated and having eaten a healthy meal beforehand.
  3. The donation process typically takes about an hour, including registration, screening, and the actual donation.
  4. After donating, relax and enjoy refreshments while your body replenishes the blood.
